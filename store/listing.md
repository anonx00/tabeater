## Detailed Description

PHANTOM TABS - Tactical Tab Intelligence System

Take control of your browser tabs with AI-powered intelligence. PHANTOM TABS uses cutting-edge AI to help you organize, analyze, and manage your tabs effortlessly.

KEY FEATURES

Smart Tab Organization
Automatically group tabs by domain with one click. Keep your browser organized without manual effort.

Duplicate Detection
Find and close duplicate tabs instantly. Free up memory and reduce clutter.

AI-Powered Analysis
Get intelligent insights about your open tabs. The AI analyzes your browsing session and suggests optimizations.

Interactive AI Chat
Ask questions about your tabs in natural language. "Which tabs are related to my project?" - the AI understands context.

Tactical Side Panel
A command center view of all your tabs. Switch between domain-grouped view and full list. Real-time updates as you browse.

Privacy-First AI
Uses Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano AI when available - your data never leaves your device. Optional cloud API fallback for older systems.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Click the PHANTOM TABS icon to see your current tabs
2. Use quick actions: Organize, Duplicates, Analyze
3. Open the side panel for the full tactical view
4. Configure AI settings in the options page

PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED

- Tabs: Required to list and manage your tabs
- Tab Groups: Required for smart tab grouping
- Storage: Saves your preferences locally
- Side Panel: Enables the tactical command center
- Scripting: Extracts page content for AI analysis
- Active Tab: Accesses current tab information

PERFECT FOR

- Researchers with many reference tabs
- Developers juggling multiple projects
- Anyone who tends to accumulate tabs
- Power users who want AI assistance

No account required. No data collection. Your browsing stays private.
